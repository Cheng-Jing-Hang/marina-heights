Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

1. Where is Marina Heights located?

Marina Heights is located near the coast, just a short walk from the LRT station and close to major shopping centres.

2. What type of units are available at Marina Heights?

We offer a range of unit types including 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, and 3-bedroom units with sea or city views.

3. How can I schedule a tour or viewing of a unit?

You can book a tour through our website's Meet Our Agent section and click "Contact Agent" or call our management office during working hours.

4. Is Marina Heights a freehold or leasehold property?

Marina Heights is a freehold based condominium.

Visitor Information

5. Are visitors allowed to use the condo facilities?

Facilities such as the pool and gym are reserved for residents. Visitors may use certain amenities only if accompanied by a resident.

6. Is visitor parking available?

Yes, designated visitor parking bays are available. All visitors must register when applying a visit pass.

7. How do I apply a visit pass?

At the home page, click "Apply for Visit Pass", then fill in your details.

8. Can visitors book the facilities?

Only Multipurpose hall is allowed to be booked by visitors. Kindly contact our management office during office hours for more details.

Resident Services

9. How do I book condo facilities like the BBQ pit or multi-purpose hall?

Bookings can be made through the Resident Portal or by visiting the management office. Some facilities may require a deposit and/or fees.

10. Who do I contact for maintenance issues in my unit?

Please submit a report via the Resident Portal or call our management office during office hours.

11. How can I update my contact details or car plate number?

You can update your details through the Resident Portal or by submitting a form at the management office.

12. Is there a recycling or waste management schedule?

Yes, recycling bins are available at every floor. General waste is collected daily and recyclables every Tuesday and Friday.

Safety & Security

13. Are there CCTV cameras in common areas?

Yes, CCTV is installed throughout key common areas for your safety.

14. What should I do in case of an emergency?

Call the emergency number listed in the Resident Handbook or notify the security guardhouse immediately.

15. Are deliveries allowed to be sent directly to units?

For security, deliveries are accepted at the guardhouse or lobby and must be collected by the resident.

Payment & Fees

16. How do I pay my maintenance fees?

Fees can be paid online via the Resident Portal or at the management office. E-receipts are issued for every payment.

17. Are there penalties for late payment of maintenance fees?

Yes, a late fee is charged as per the MC's rules and regulations. Please refer to the Resident Handbook page for details.
